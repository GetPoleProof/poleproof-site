Privacy Policy

Draft — last edited July 2026. Not yet reviewed by counsel.
This policy is a working draft pending attorney review, and is provided here so visitors and concierge audit clients can see it before sending any documents. It will be revised as that review is completed.

What we collect

When you email PoleProof or send documents for a concierge audit, we collect what you send us directly: your name, company, contact details, and the documents themselves (invoices, estimates, and pole attachment agreements). We do not use tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or third-party ad tech on this site.

How we use it

  • To perform the audit you requested and return findings to you.
  • To respond to your questions and follow up about an audit in progress.
  • To improve PoleProof's internal benchmark data, in de-identified and aggregated form only, so future audits benefit from patterns observed across clients.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or rent your information to any third party.
  • We don't share your specific invoices, agreements, or findings with anyone outside PoleProof, except at your direction (for example, if you ask us to send a report to your counsel).
  • We don't use your documents for any purpose beyond producing your audit and the aggregated benchmark data described above.

How long we keep it

We retain client documents and findings for as long as needed to support the audit relationship and any resulting dispute, and delete or archive them on request once that need has passed.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it, by emailing hello@getpoleproof.com. We'll honor deletion requests except where we need to retain something for an active dispute or a legal obligation.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@getpoleproof.com.
